Addictive offline monster game for those who like to play casual monster shooting games without internet.

Monsters attack the Earth from the sky. You are the Monster liquidator and you have 10 shots. Can you defeat monsters and destroy them all?

Your task is to shoot monsters and destroy them all. For each destroyed monster, you get an additional shot as a reward.
After destroying all the monsters, you go to the next level. Each new level adds a new monster.
This monster game has 10 levels.

If you like casual games where you have to shoot monsters and destroy them, then this monster shooting game is for you.

Good luck!
