Stay informed about Bitcoin price dynamics with the Bitcoin Neratan app.

This app combines BTC/USD price charts with Bitcoin news sentiment analysis to provide you with a comprehensive view of market trends.

Features:

- BTC/USD price charts for various timeframes: M, W, D3, D, H12, H8, H6, H4.
- Analysis of Bitcoin news to gauge positive or negative market sentiment.
- Observe the correlation between news sentiment shifts and corresponding price fluctuations.
- Empower your trading decisions by understanding the influence of news sentiment on Bitcoin price movements.

Whether you're a seasoned trader or a curious investor, the Bitcoin Neratan app provides valuable information to help you navigate the dynamic Bitcoin market.
