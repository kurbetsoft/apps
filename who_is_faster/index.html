Apps / Who is Faster
Compete on reaction speed from 1 to 4 players and determine who is faster.

Get it on Google Play
Compete with your friends or yourself in exciting blitz tournaments for reaction speed, where your task is to instantly press your button faster than everyone else and win!
All results are recorded in the leaderboard. Rise above, surpassing previous achievements, to claim the top spot in the reaction speed ranking.

How to play:

- Choose the number of players (from 1 to 4).
- Each player is provided with their own button of a specific color.
- Await the flash of the light bulb accompanied by a sound signal.
- Prove who is faster by being the first to press own button after the signal.
- Results are saved in the leaderboard for later comparison of reaction speeds.

Show your skill in reaction speed and determine who is faster among the participants in the game.
