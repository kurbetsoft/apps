What are runes?

25 runes make up the alphabet of the ancient Germans, Scandinavians and Anglo-Saxons.
Each of the 24 runes (plus one empty) has its own symbol, the carrier of the message that needs to be revealed to find out the answer.
In medieval Europe, druids, sorcerers and shamans used runes to communicate with the Spirit.
