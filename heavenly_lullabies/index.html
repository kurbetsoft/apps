A Collection of Lullabies for Peaceful Baby Sleep and Family Relaxation

The Heavenly Lullabies app offers a selection of gentle and soothing melodies that help babies fall asleep faster and create a cozy atmosphere for the entire family. All tracks are available offline – you can listen anytime, anywhere, without an internet connection.
The app supports background playback and features an automatic sleep timer, making it especially convenient for everyday use.

Easy Control and Favorites Always at Your Fingertips

The interface is intuitive and simple – easily manage playback, add your favorite tracks to Favorites, and quickly find them the next time you open the app. You can also reorder tracks, enable repeat, and shuffle playback.

A Variety of Musical Styles and Moods

The collection includes lullabies and calming compositions carefully chosen to create a soft and comforting atmosphere. Find music across a range of genres and moods – from classical to modern – suitable for both babies and the whole family.

Offline Access and Energy Efficiency

All melodies are stored directly on your device, so the app works without internet access. Thanks to optimized energy consumption, music can play for extended periods without draining your battery or overloading your device.

Flexible Sleep Timer

Set your preferred playback duration – from a few minutes to several hours. The timer automatically stops the music once your baby falls asleep, helping save battery life and ensuring a peaceful night’s rest.

Background Playback and Locked Screen Support

Music continues to play even when the app is minimized or your screen is locked – so the process of falling asleep and relaxing is never interrupted.

Gentle Calmness for Your Home

Create a cozy, harmonious space for your family with soothing melodies that encourage peaceful sleep for babies and relaxation for adults.
Heavenly Lullabies fills your evenings with calm, helps your little ones drift off easily, and gives you moments of tranquility.

Let every sleep begin with gentle music and end with a happy smile!
