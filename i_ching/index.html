Divination by the Book of Changes

Mentally formulate the question that currently interests you the most and the answer to which you expect from the Book of Changes.
Do not ask the same question again, even if you are not satisfied with the answer or it seems too gloomy to you.

Throw three coins in turn

This must be done six times to get six lines and make a hexagram.
If two or three coins land heads up, a solid line is drawn (Yang). If two or three coins land up tails, a broken line is drawn (Yin).
