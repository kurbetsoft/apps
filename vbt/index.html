Application for boxing training and boxing learning at home.
Virtual boxing trainer for those who want to learn boxing at home.

The application has three modes. The first is an interactive boxing book with explanatory videos, a self-tutorial. The second is boxing training with a timer and exercise visualization. The third is a boxing school, where video lessons are presented with basic techniques, typical mistakes and boxing exercises.

Boxing self-tutorial

Theoretical part. In the boxing book you can get acquainted with the boxing warm-up, a set of exercises in front of a mirror, punches and defense techniques, characteristics of tactical actions, a set of exercises in pairs, exercises for developing a sense of distance and paws exercises.

Boxing training

Practical part. In this mode, you can train boxing at home, on your own or in pairs.
It is also possible to adjust the duration of the boxing training and choose the exercises you need from the categories: warm-up at the mirror, warm-up on the go, boxing school in front of the mirror, warm-up in pairs, exercises in pairs to develop the distance, tasks in pairs, paws exercises.

Boxing school

Practical part. Learning and training through video lessons on basic skills, including proper fist positioning and elbow placement, as well as exercises for body defense, wrist strengthening, and increasing punching power.
Detailed analysis of typical mistakes made by novice boxers.
